Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 11, 2026

StudyTrack ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information when you use our mobile application, StudyTrack (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

a. Personal Data

The App is designed to function without requiring you to create an account. We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number directly.

b. Usage Data (Local)

We store your study sessions, focus time, and achievements locally on your device using an internal database. This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

c. Third-Party Data Collection (Ads)

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use information to provide personalized ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (e.g., Android Advertising ID or iOS IDFA).
  • Your IP address.
  • Approximate location data (if permitted by your device).
  • App usage statistics related to ad interactions.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information collected to provide and maintain the App's functionality, personalize your experience, display relevant advertisements, and analyze App performance.

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you.

  • Google Play Services
  • AdMob

4. Advertising and Opt-Out

You can opt-out of personalized advertising through your device settings.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at ddtechstudio8@gmail.com.
