Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 30, 2026

StudyTrack ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information when you use the StudyTrack mobile application ("App"). StudyTrack contains no advertisements, does not require an account, and we never sell your data.

1. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information

The App does not require you to create an account. We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location.

b. Study Data

Your study sessions, focus time, subjects, goals, achievements, and other study information are stored locally on your device. This information is not transmitted to our servers.

c. Anonymous Usage Analytics

We use PostHog to collect anonymous usage analytics that help us understand how the App is used and improve its performance and features. Examples of events we may collect include starting or completing a study session, viewing screens, using widgets, changing the app theme, completing onboarding, and interacting with app features.

These analytics are associated with a randomly generated application identifier. We do not send your name, email address, phone number, study notes, subject names, goals, or other personally identifying information to PostHog.

2. How We Use Information

Anonymous analytics are used solely to:

  • Understand how features are used.
  • Improve the App and user experience.
  • Identify and fix issues.
  • Guide future feature development.

Your locally stored study data is used only to provide the App's functionality on your device.

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Services
  • PostHog (Anonymous Product Analytics)

4. Your Choices

Analytics collected through PostHog use a random application identifier rather than directly identifying information. You can reset this identifier by clearing the App's data or reinstalling the App.

If analytics consent is required by applicable law in your region, we will request your permission before collecting analytics data.

5. Data Security

Data transmitted for analytics is encrypted in transit using secure network connections. We take reasonable measures to protect the information collected by the App.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
