Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 14, 2026

StudyTrack ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information when you use the StudyTrack mobile application ("App"). StudyTrack contains no advertisements and we never sell your data. The App works fully offline with no account; the optional cloud backup & sync feature uses your own Google account.

1. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information

The App does not require you to create an account. We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location on our servers — in fact, we operate no servers. If you choose to enter an optional display name, it is stored locally on your device and is included in your personal Google Drive backup only if you enable cloud sync (see section 1c). It is never sent to us.

b. Study Data

Your study sessions, focus time, subjects, goals, achievements, and other study information are stored locally on your device. This information is not transmitted to our servers, and stays on your device unless you choose to enable cloud backup & sync (see section 1c).

c. Cloud Backup & Sync (Google Drive)

StudyTrack offers an optional, premium cloud backup & sync feature. It is turned off by default and is only activated when you sign in with your Google account. When enabled:

  • Your backup is stored in a private, hidden folder inside your own Google Drive (using Google's restricted drive.appdata scope). This folder is accessible only to StudyTrack — the App cannot see, read, or access any other part of your Google Drive.
  • We operate no servers and cannot access, read, or browse your backup. It lives entirely within your own Google Drive.
  • The backup includes your study sessions, subject names, your preferences and settings, and your display name (if you set one). A random, non-identifying device ID is included so your data can be merged across your devices.
  • Your data is transmitted and stored securely by Google Drive (encrypted in transit and at rest). StudyTrack does not add its own separate encryption layer.
  • You can turn sync off, and delete your cloud backup, at any time from Settings → Backup & Sync. Turning it off keeps all of your data local to your device.

A manual, fully offline backup option (exporting and importing a file) is also available and never uses the network or a Google account.

d. Anonymous Usage Analytics

We use PostHog to collect anonymous usage analytics that help us understand how the App is used and improve its performance and features. Examples of events we may collect include starting or completing a study session, viewing screens, using widgets, changing the app theme, completing onboarding, and interacting with app features.

These analytics are associated with a randomly generated application identifier. We do not send your name, email address, phone number, study notes, subject names, goals, or other personally identifying information to PostHog.

2. How We Use Information

Anonymous analytics are used solely to:

  • Understand how features are used.
  • Improve the App and user experience.
  • Identify and fix issues.
  • Guide future feature development.

Your locally stored study data is used only to provide the App's functionality on your device.

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google Drive — only if you enable cloud backup & sync; used to store your backup in your own Google Drive.
  • Google Play Billing — used only for the one-time in-app purchase; payments are handled entirely by Google and no payment or card data reaches the App.
  • PostHog (Anonymous Product Analytics)

4. Your Choices

Analytics collected through PostHog use a random application identifier rather than directly identifying information. You can reset this identifier by clearing the App's data or reinstalling the App.

If analytics consent is required by applicable law in your region, we will request your permission before collecting analytics data.

Cloud backup & sync is optional and off by default. You can enable or disable it, and delete your Google Drive backup, at any time from Settings → Backup & Sync. Turning it off keeps all of your data local to your device.

5. Data Security

Data transmitted for analytics is encrypted in transit using secure network connections. If you enable cloud backup & sync, your backup is stored in a private, app-only folder in your own Google Drive and is protected by Google's encryption in transit and at rest. We take reasonable measures to protect the information collected by the App.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
